Welcome to Physics Mythbusters!

Physics is all around us, from the smartphones in our pockets to the satellites above us. However, it remains one of the most misunderstood sciences, leaving information vulnerable to misuse and exploitation to sway public opinion.

We aim to do three things:

  • Identify misinformation and common misconceptions
  • Explain its errors
  • Rebuild understanding of the concept

Whether its blackholes opening gateways to another universe, or magically healing quantum crystals, we strive to separate fact from fiction to provide YOU with clarity.

Here you can find:

  • Clear scientific explanations
  • Evidence-based analysis of claims
  • Advice on spotting pseudoscience
