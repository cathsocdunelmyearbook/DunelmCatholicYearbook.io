Welcome to the Yearbook

In 2025, after some time-wasting on Canva during the Summer, a somewhat lamented hope of some members was brought to fruition in the form of a Catholic Society Yearbook. The hope not only was to coherently document and explain in reasonable depth, the ocurrences in the Chaplaincy and Society life- but in addition, to informativley bridge together generations of alumni and students, and to breathe hope into the year that would follow. May the Lord and the intercessions of Our Lady, and St. Cuthbert be upon us.
